Light Experiment





Step 1: Fill a glass with water.

Step 2: Hold the tip of the pencil and insert it into the water

Step 3: Look at the pencil through the side of the glass, what happened

Why did the pencil bend?

The pencil looks to be bent because refraction is taking place. Refraction is a concept that refers to how a wave travels through different mediums of varying densities. Here the glass, air, and water are the medium, and light waves are traveling through them. Did you know light waves travel faster through the air than water or glass? This is because air is a less dense material compared to glass or water. So as the light waves enter glass and water, they slow down and bend into the water. Now that the light waves are no longer traveling at the same angle, the pencil looks bent.
